About Us

Venezuelan Recipes CookBook, is what you can call a collaborative website, where you can create your own venezuelan food recipes, upload them and share your culinary skills with everybody. You can also avail of the recipes already uploaded on the site. You can learn and try new dishes from a tropical country such as Venezuela, wherein, food is one of its main features.

All our recipes are user-created. The point of this initiative is to broaden the knowledge about Venezuelan dishes. Nowadays, you can see these recipes being cooked anywhere in the world, thanks to the large exodus of Venezuelan people across the globe. You can find the necessary ingredients in several stores, some particular ones you'll find in foreign-goods shops.

Remember... All you need to do to create your own recipes, comment on existing ones and like them is register and if you're already registered then login. That's it! We hope you enjoy this website and make the most of it for your future culinary endeavors.
